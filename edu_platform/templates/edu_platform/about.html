Frequently Asked Questions

To sign up for an account, simply click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner of the homepage. You will be prompted to provide your email address, create a password, and fill out some basic information. Once you submit the form, your account will be created, and you can start exploring our platform.
Our platform offers a wide range of courses covering various subjects, including programming, business, arts, and more. You can browse the available courses by clicking on the "Courses" tab in the navigation menu. We regularly update our course catalog to provide you with the latest and most relevant learning opportunities.
To enroll in a course, simply navigate to the course page and click on the "Enroll Now" button. If you haven't already signed in, you will be prompted to sign in or create an account. Once you're logged in, you can proceed with the enrollment process and start learning right away.
Currently, our platform does not support offline access to course materials. However, you can access the course content online from any device with an internet connection. We're constantly working on improving our platform, and offline access may be considered in future updates.
If you encounter any issues or have questions about our platform, you can contact our support team by emailing support@learningwave.com or by filling out the contact form on our website. Our support team will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible and assist you with any problems you may have.
